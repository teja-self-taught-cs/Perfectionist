My Story

The Self-Taught Developer

I'm a self-taught computer science enthusiast, always eager to dive deeper into the vast world of technology. Through my own initiative and curiosity, I've been on a journey of continuous learning, devouring online resources, tutorials, and textbooks to expand my knowledge base. Alongside this self-directed study, I've sought out internships to gain practical experience and insight into the industry. Building projects has become a cornerstone of my learning process. Each project presents new challenges and opportunities to apply what I've learned, solidifying concepts and pushing me to explore beyond my comfort zone. Whether it's a small utility app or a more complex software solution, each project fuels my passion for problem-solving and innovation. While the road to proficiency in computer science is a winding one, I embrace the challenges and setbacks as opportunities for growth. With each line of code I write and every project I complete, I'm one step closer to realising my aspirations in the field.
